A Legacy, Continued

GoodbodyBucks is inspired by a handwritten board from a previous generation.

The original hand-drawn schedule that managed 9 children in the Goodbody household

Managing Nine Children

Before apps and smartphones, a simple hand-drawn board kept an entire household running. Nine children learned responsibility through a visible system where everyone knew their role.

The board showed who cleaned dishes, who had ice rights for the week, and when each child contributed to the family. It wasn't about control‚Äîit was about clarity.

The tools have changed. The lesson hasn't.

Responsibility builds confidence. Clarity builds trust. And when kids understand the system, they rise to meet it.
